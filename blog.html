OUR RANKINGS

Chandigarh Group of Colleges, Landran has so far won numerous accolades and awards under various categories. In 2021 too, the college managed to maintain the lead. The recent NIRF ranking has added another glorious achievement to our hall of fame. The National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) under the Ministry of Education conducts surveys, and it ranks different institutions based on a multitude of parameters as ‘Teaching, Learning and Resources’, ‘Research and Professional Practices’, ‘Graduation Outcomes’, ‘Outreach and Inclusivity’ and ‘Perception’.In the latest rankings by NIRF, Chandigarh College of Pharmacy (CCP) and Chandigarh Engineering College (CEC) have made a mark for themselves. In the Pharmacy category under “NIRF Ranking 2021- All India Rankings”, the Chandigarh College of Pharmacy has been ranked at the 63rd position among Pharmacy Institutions In India. On the other hand, in the Engineering category, Chandigarh Engineering College got placed at the 147th rank pan India.

ABOUT US

Chandigarh Group of Colleges, Landran has become a synonym for excellence. Situated on National Highway 205A, CGC Landran offers top-tier educational facilities. CGC Landran, was established in 2001 to bridge the gap between inquisitive minds and the education they deserve. Initially, Engineering was the flagship programme at CGC Landran and with time, the university introduced other programmes in the curriculum.
